\section{Problem}
If (k-3),(2k+1),(4k+3) are three consecutive terms of A.P., find the valuee of k



\section{Solution}
let a= k-3, b= 2k+1, c =4k+3 are three consecutive terms of A.P.\\

common difference between consecutive terms of A.P. is same\\
\begin{align}
(b-a) & = (c-b)\\
(2k+1)-(k-3)& =(4k+3)-(2k+1)\\
2k+1 -k+3 & = 4k+3 -2k-1\\
k + 4 & = 2k + 2\\
-k  & =  -2\\
k & = 2
\end{align}

\section{Answer}
k is equal to 2
